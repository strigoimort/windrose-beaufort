Wind Rose Chart with Highcharts

A wind rose is a graphical tool used to represent the distribution of wind direction and speed over time. It is widely used in meteorology, oceanography, and other fields to analyze wind patterns and help in decision-making.

You can upload a CSV or Excel file containing wind direction and speed data. The chart will be generated based on the Beaufort scale, which categorizes wind speeds into different levels.

Please upload your wind data file (CSV or Excel format). The file should contain wind speed and wind direction columns.
